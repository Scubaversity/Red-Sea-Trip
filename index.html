Red Sea Livaboard Trip

Fri, 03 - Mon 13 August

The Diving Mecca Of The World!

Diving in the Red Sea has to be on the top of any Scuba Divers bucket list.
Egypt's reefs are brimming over with life, bright corals and clouds of fish with a kaleidoscope of colour. Stating here that the waters are calm and clear is a bit of an understatement.

There are sites suitable for all levels and some good areas to learn as well as exciting open ocean sites for the more advanced diver.

Famous for iconic ancient Egyptian sites, Egypt excites the imagination like no other destination. Underwater Egypt does not disappoint either with gorgeous reefs, great wrecks and hugely diverse marine life.
